Potato Salad

Potato Salad is the best food!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Celery
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Onion
  • Relish

Step By Step

  1. Cut the potatoes in small pieces and cook in salty water until they are soft
  2. Mince the celery, onion and relish. Combine all three, adding the mayonnaise and mustard
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Combine the cooked potatoes with the other ingredients and mix
