Lasagna

Lasagna is the best food!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Step By Step

  1. Use the beef to make minced meat
  2. Cook the meat and add seasoning
  3. Add the sauce to the meat
  4. Cook the noodles in the water. Don't forget to add salt to the water
  5. Build the lasagna, alternating between noodle, meat sauce and cheese
  6. Once combined, finish cooking in the oven
